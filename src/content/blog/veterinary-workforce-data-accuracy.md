## The Hidden Cost of Inaccurate Workforce Data

Multi-site veterinary operators face a fundamental challenge: hiring decisions are made without knowing the true addressable talent pool. State licensing boards operate independently across all 50 states, each with different data formats, update frequencies, and accuracy levels.

## Why State Board Data Fails Operators

State licensing boards provide the foundation for veterinary workforce intelligence, but they come with critical gaps:

- **Outdated Contact Information**: Approximately 30% of "active" veterinarian licenses contain outdated contact information
- **Incomplete Employment Status**: Licensing data rarely indicates current employment or availability
- **Geographic Ambiguity**: License addresses don't reflect actual practice locations
- **Credential Fragmentation**: Specialties and certifications exist across multiple disconnected systems

## The Impact on Hiring Velocity

When workforce data is incomplete or inaccurate, operators experience:

- Roles remaining open for months due to incorrect addressable market assumptions
- Recruiters spending time on candidates who are unreachable or already employed
- Territory expansion plans based on flawed talent availability projections
- Competitive disadvantage against operators with better market intelligence
